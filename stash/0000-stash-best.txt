The first generation is a single, pre-made character.
The only things that change are the different movesets.
The character chooses which move to use next based off which moveset statements are both true and most specific.
If multiple moveset statements are equally specific, the higher one is used.
Fallbacks (statements that are simply "true") have no value.
Nothing in the battles are random, the only random factor is the mutations to each character (other than the first).
These are the best characters in each generation.
